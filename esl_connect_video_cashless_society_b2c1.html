Context

Situation: A city council is debating whether buses, public offices and local markets should become cashless.

Student A

You support the move. Explain the benefits for efficiency, cost and fraud prevention.

Student B

You are concerned. Explain the risks for privacy, elderly people, people without bank accounts and remote communities.
