Comprehension

Click each question to reveal the answer.

What four major transformations in healthcare are mentioned at the beginning?
Medicine will become more precise, value will shape care delivery, patients will become empowered consumers, and digitalization will transform every part of healthcare.
What is a digital twin in this context?
It is a customized computational model of a patient that can help doctors anticipate illness, guide therapy options and predict procedure outcomes.
How will precision medicine help radiologists?
It will help them make highly specific diagnoses with fewer unwarranted variations.
What will AI-powered decision models use to improve diagnostic accuracy?
They will use vast population health data.
How will healthcare become more anticipatory?
It will be organized around the patient’s medical conditions and available around the clock.
How will patient experience improve?
Interactions will become more convenient, personalized and compassionate.

B2/C1 Grammar: Future Predictions with Will and Future Passive

This grammar section practises formal future predictions often used in technology, healthcare and business contexts.

UsePatternExample
Predictionwill + verbHealthcare will undergo dramatic transformations.
Future passivewill be + past participleCare will be organized around patients’ needs.
Resultwill enable + object + to + verbPrecision medicine will enable doctors to make better diagnoses.
Transformationwill transform + nounDigitalization will transform healthcare.
