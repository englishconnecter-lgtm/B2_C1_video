B2/C1 Grammar: Gerunds and Infinitives for Consumer Behaviour

This section practises gerunds and infinitives for discussing buying decisions, identity and brand influence.

Consumers often choose ___ more for a brand because it expresses identity.

Some people avoid ___ that brands influence them.

Brands can help people ___ who they are.

Gerund or Infinitive in Context

People may decide a product because of its image.

Brand loyalty can involve a product publicly.

Consumers sometimes fail subconscious influences.

Companies keep stories about identity.

Options

Useful Patterns

PatternExample
decide + infinitiveConsumers decide to buy a certain brand.
avoid + gerundPeople avoid admitting that brands influence them.
involve + gerundBrand loyalty can involve defending a product.
help + object + infinitiveBrands help people express identity.
Task: Explain why people buy brands. Use at least three gerunds and three infinitives.

Role Play

Student A: Brand strategist

Explain how identity, emotion and affiliation can make the brand stronger.
