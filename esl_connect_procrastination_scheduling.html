Procrastination, Scheduling and Interruptions

B2/C1 lesson on prioritising, context switching, attention and productivity systems.

Open video on YouTube

Lesson Overview

Students watch a video comparing computer scheduling with human time management. The lesson explores procrastination, prioritising, interruptions, context switching, interrupt coalescing and ways to reclaim attention.

Before you watch

  1. When you have too many tasks, do you spend more time planning or actually doing?
  2. How often do messages, emails or notifications interrupt your work?
  3. Can being perfectly organised ever make someone less productive?

What does a scheduler decide?
