Comprehension 1

Why is AI changing the energy conversation?
AI requires huge computing power, which increases electricity demand from data centers.
How much more power can AI require at individual data centers?
The video says AI can escalate power demand by a factor of ten or even 100.
What does the Department of Energy estimate about global electricity demand?
It estimates global electricity demand could rise from a third to three quarters by 2050.
Why did countries start looking at nuclear energy again?
Energy prices, geopolitical volatility and concerns about energy security pushed countries to reconsider nuclear power.
Why is nuclear different from solar and wind?
Solar and wind are intermittent, while nuclear can provide 24/7 baseload emissions-free energy.

Which tech companies are mentioned as investing in or exploring nuclear power?
Amazon, Microsoft, Google and Meta are mentioned, along with Bill Gates-founded TerraPower.
What is Amazon doing?
Amazon agreed to a nuclear power purchase agreement and is investing over $500 million in nuclear projects.
What is Microsoft doing?
Microsoft is working with Constellation Energy to reopen the Three Mile Island nuclear facility with planned upgrades.
What are SMRs?
SMRs are small modular reactors that are smaller, faster to build and potentially more cost-effective than traditional reactors.
What challenge still remains for SMRs in the U.S.?
There are currently no operational SMRs in the U.S., and regulatory approval remains a challenge.

B2/C1 Grammar: Future Predictions and Passive Reporting

This grammar section practises formal language for discussing future energy trends and reported investment plans.

FunctionPatternExample
Future predictioncould / is expected to + verbElectricity demand could rise significantly by 2050.
Reported plansbe working to / be planning toMicrosoft is working to reopen Three Mile Island.
Passive reportingbe slated to + verbSMRs are slated to open in 2030 and beyond.
Purposein order to / so thatTech companies need steady power so that AI can scale.

Grammar Exercise 3: Sentence Transformations

Use “could”.
Electricity demand may increase sharply in the future.
Use “is working to”.
Microsoft wants to reopen Three Mile Island.
Use “are slated to”.
SMRs are planned to open after 2030.
Use “so that”.
Companies need clean steady power. They want AI data centers to operate continuously.
