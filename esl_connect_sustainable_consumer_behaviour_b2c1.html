Sustainable Consumer Behaviour: From Intention to Action

B2/C1 business lesson on sustainability, consumer behaviour, segmentation and strategy.

Open video on YouTube

Lesson Overview

Students watch a business sustainability video about consumer behaviour change. The lesson explores sustainable shopping, misconceptions about consumers, growing markets, segmentation, test-and-learn marketing and how companies can help people make better choices.

Before you watch

  1. Do consumers really want to shop more sustainably?
  2. What stops people from choosing sustainable products?
  3. Should companies try to change consumer behaviour?
