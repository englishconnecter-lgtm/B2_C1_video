Lesson Aim

Students watch a video about how big data and analytics have transformed professional basketball, especially the Houston Rockets, and discuss data-driven decision-making in sport and business.

Before watching

  2. Should coaches trust data or instinct more?
  3. What other industries use analytics to gain an edge?
