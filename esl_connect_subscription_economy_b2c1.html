B2/C1 Grammar: Future Continuous and Future Perfect

This grammar section practises future forms for predicting ongoing payments and completed actions.

UsePatternExample
Future continuous: action in progress in the futurewill be + -ingBy next year, more devices will be charging monthly fees.
Future continuous: expected future situationwill be + -ingConsumers will be paying for more connected services.
Future perfect: completed before a future timewill have + past participleBy the end of the year, I will have cancelled three subscriptions.
Future perfect: result by a deadlinewill have + past participleBy next month, companies will have introduced more subscription options.
