AI Layoffs and the Future of Jobs

B2/C1 lesson on automation, layoffs, AI-proof careers, adaptable skills and the future of work.

Open video on YouTube

Lesson Overview

Students watch a news interview about AI-related layoffs and the future of work. The lesson focuses on which jobs may be most exposed to automation, which skills are safer, and why adaptability matters more than learning one narrow technical skill.

Before you watch

  1. Which jobs do you think AI can replace most easily?
  2. Which jobs need too much human interaction to be fully automated?
  3. Is coding still a safe career choice?

B2/C1 Grammar: Risk, Prediction and Advice

This grammar section practises language for discussing job risk and career advice in the age of AI.

FunctionPatternExample
Riskbe at risk of + -ingAccountants are at risk of being replaced by AI.
Predictionbe likely to + verbJobs with human interaction are likely to survive longer.
Abilitybe able to + verbAdaptable workers will be able to change careers.
Adviceshould / need to + verbStudents should learn how to learn.
